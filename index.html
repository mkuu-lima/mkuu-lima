Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 December 2025

Thank you for using Mkuu Lima — the all-in-one farming app built for Ugandan farmers.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, phone number, email, profile photo
  • Farm Details: Location, farm size, crops, livestock
  • Listings & Transactions: Items you sell or buy
  • Photos: Images of crops, animals, or documents you upload
  • Device & Usage Data: Device type, app crashes, how you use the app
  • Location: Only if you enable GPS for weather or nearby buyers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Connect buyers and sellers of produce and livestock
  • Help you find tractor rental and expert advice
  • Send order updates, offers, and weather alerts
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Prevent fraud and keep the platform safe

3. How We Share Your Information

We never sell your personal data. We only share when necessary:

  • With other users during transactions (e.g., buyer sees your phone number)
  • With trusted partners (payment providers, cloud hosting)
  • If required by Ugandan law or authorities

4. Data Security

We use bank-level encryption and regular security checks to protect your data. However, no app is 100% secure — we continuously work to keep it safe.

5. Your Rights

You can always:

  • Access or delete your data
  • Turn off location services
  • Delete your account (email us)

6. Children

Mkuu Lima is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Phone: +256 700 000 000 (Uganda)

By using Mkuu Lima, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Thank you for trusting us on your farming journey.
