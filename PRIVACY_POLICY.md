# Privacy Policy for Mkuu Lima

**Effective Date:** 1 December 2025  
**Last Updated:** 1 December 2025

Thank you for using **Mkuu Lima** (“the App”), a mobile application designed to help farmers in Uganda and beyond buy inputs, sell produce, access services, and manage their farms efficiently.

We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the App.

### 1. Information We Collect

#### a. Information You Provide Directly
- Account information (name, phone number, email, profile photo)
- Farm details (location, size, crops, livestock)
- Listings you create (produce for sale, services requested, etc.)
- Messages or support requests you send
- Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers)

#### b. Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Log data (IP address, app crashes, usage patterns)
- Location data (if you enable GPS for weather or market features)
- Analytics data (how you navigate and use the app)

#### c. Photos & Media
- Images you upload (e.g., of crops, livestock, or farm documents)

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:
- Provide and improve the App’s core features
- Match buyers and sellers of produce and livestock
- Connect you with service providers (tractor hire, consultants, etc.)
- Send important notifications (order updates, offers, weather alerts)
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

### 3. How We Share Your Information
We do **not** sell your personal data. We may share it only in these cases:
- With other users when you post listings or transact (e.g., buyer sees your phone number if you allow it)
- With trusted service providers (payment gateways, cloud hosting, analytics)
- For legal reasons (if required by law enforcement or court order)
- In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

### 4. Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

### 5. Your Rights & Choices
You have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data
- Withdraw consent (where applicable)
- Disable location services in your device settings
- Delete your account (contact support@mkuulima.com)

### 6. Data Retention
We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above or as required by law. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 24 months.

### 7. Children’s Privacy
Mkuu Lima is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

### 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes inside the app or via email.
